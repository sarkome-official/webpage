Discovery Pipeline

Our approach integrates high-throughput wet lab data with causal representation learning to build the first comprehensive map of disease etiology.

PHASE 01 — DATA

Multi-omics Ingestion

Aggregating genomic, transcriptomic, and proteomic datasets from diverse patient cohorts to establish a ground truth baseline.

PHASE 02 — MODEL

Causal Graph Learning

Applying neuro-symbolic architectures to infer causal relationships between genes, proteins, and phenotypic outcomes, going beyond mere correlation.

PHASE 03 — VALIDATE

In Silico Perturbation

Simulating billions of therapeutic interventions digitally to identify high-probability candidates before wet lab synthesis.
